About Us

In 2017, My Fitness started as an enterprise with the intention of providing quality fitness equipment to the Pakistan market. In 2018, we evolved into a private limited company with over 15 employees, catering to the latest trends in the fitness industry.

We proudly deal in brands such as Techno, Life Fitness, Fitness First, Precor, and Body Strong, providing quality gym equipment ranging from heavy-duty cardiovascular equipment to dumbbells & accessories.

Our Vision

We aim to maintain creative and innovative product development methods, delivering quality products of the highest standard. Designing and R&D are carefully done and scrutinized by our experts to ensure top-notch quality. We plan strategic alliances with suppliers to offer high-quality products at affordable prices.

Our Clients

Since 2017, we have been working with multiple clients, including Armed Forces, Multinational Companies, Telcos, and Private Companies.

Our Services

  • Servicing and maintenance of gym equipment.
  • Gym proposals (Layout designing and planning).
  • Gym installation.
  • Gym instructors (Personal Trainers/Group Training).
  • Corporate fitness programs.
